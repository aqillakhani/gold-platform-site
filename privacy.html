Privacy Policy

Gold Platform
Last updated: March 13, 2026

1. Introduction

Gold Platform ("we", "our", "the Service") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our content management platform.

2. Information We Collect

When you use the Service, we may collect:

  • Account Information: Social media account identifiers and OAuth access tokens that you authorize through each platform's official login process.
  • Content Data: Video files, captions, hashtags, and metadata that you submit for publishing.
  • Usage Data: Publishing history, post performance metrics, and scheduling preferences.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Publish content to your authorized social media accounts
  • Retrieve performance metrics for your published content
  • Manage your posting schedule
  • Improve the functionality of the Service

4. Data Storage and Security

Access tokens and credentials are stored securely in encrypted environment files on private infrastructure. We do not store your social media passwords. All API communications use HTTPS encryption.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your content is transmitted only to the social media platforms you have explicitly authorized.

6. Third-Party Services

The Service integrates with the following third-party platforms through their official APIs:

  • TikTok (Content Posting API)
  • YouTube (Data API v3)
  • Meta/Facebook (Graph API)
  • Instagram (Graph API)

Your use of these platforms is governed by their respective privacy policies.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Revoke API access at any time through each platform's settings
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
